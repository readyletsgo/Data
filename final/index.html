A Race Through Walmart

This page explores the relationship between products sold and advirtised at Walmart.


    Using a Walmart product recommendation API we are able to see what Walmart suggests for different products and through this we can see their algorithm at work. This reminded me of a game called the wiki game I used to play at school when I was bored. You would go to wikipedia on two different tabs and hit the "random" button. Then you would try to get from the first page to the second page only through clicking links. This is a similar game. You are given a random starting product and a random goal product and it is your goal to race through the product recommendations and find the goal product.
